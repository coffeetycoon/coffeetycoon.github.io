Welcome to Coffee Tycoon!

How to Play:

Build your coffee empire from the ground up! Click the coffee cup to brew coffee manually, then purchase items from the Shop to automate your production.

Quick Keys:

1 Brew House - Manual brewing
2 Shop - Purchase production items
3 Upgrades - Permanent improvements
4 Prestige - Reset for bonuses
5 Achievements - Track progress & earn rewards

Goal:

Earn coffee, unlock upgrades progressively, and reach 10 billion coffee to prestige for permanent Golden Coffee bonuses!

Coffee Tycoon v1.12

What's New in v1.12 — Pick Your Brew

  • Settings Panel: New settings button with customizable options for notifications, quick keys, number display, and progress reset
  • Save Export/Import: Export your game progress as a text string and import it on another device or browser
  • Erase Progress: Option to completely reset your game progress with confirmation
  • Extended Number Support: Number abbreviations now support up to trigintillion (10^96) for massive late-game progress

Gameplay Tips

  • Use the settings panel to customize your gameplay experience
  • Export your save regularly to backup your progress
  • The number display toggle lets you see full numbers if you prefer them over abbreviations
  • Quick keys can be customized in future updates

Previous Version: v1.11 — A Latte Noise

  • Coffee Cup Animation: The coffee cup now reacts to clicks with intensity scaling based on CPS and clicking speed
  • Sound Effects: Added sound effects for purchases and claims, preloaded and managed to prevent stacking
  • Enhanced Selling: Sell options now available up to ×100 for quick refunds
  • Shop Layout Improvements: All shop items are now vertically aligned for cleaner presentation
  • Affordable Item Indicators: Clear visual indicators highlight shop items you can currently afford
  • Achievement Pack Visibility: Achievement packs appear in the shop screen even when locked, clearly marked as such
  • Building Order Adjustment: Coffee Country and Universe now appear before Planet with recalculated progression
  • Building Achievement Packs: Each building now has its own achievement pack that unlocks with the building
  • Expanded General Achievements: General collection achievements now go up to 1,000,000 total buildings
  • Golden Coffee Nerf: Each Golden Coffee now doubles the cost of the next one, with a hard cap at 100
  • Prestige Requirement: Prestige now requires collecting at least 1 Golden Coffee
  • Clean Achievement UI: Removed all emojis from achievement packs and titles for a cleaner look

Gameplay Tips

  • Watch the coffee cup animation scale with your clicking speed and CPS
  • Use bulk selling to quickly convert buildings back to coffee when needed
  • Look for the green affordable indicators in the shop to see what you can buy
  • Building achievement packs unlock along with their respective buildings
  • Golden Coffee costs increase exponentially, plan your prestiges carefully
